# OPEN TENEBRIS - PRIVACY POLICY

**Version 1.0 | Last Updated: May 24, 2026**

## ⚡ CORE PRINCIPLE

**We do not log normal users. Period.**

Open Tenebris operates on a **privacy-by-default, surveillance-by-exception** model. Unlike major platforms that log every click, message, and IP address, we only collect data when there is a legitimate security or abuse concern.

## 📊 WHAT WE COLLECT (And Why)

| Data Type | Collected? | When | Purpose |
|-----------|------------|------|---------|
| **IP Address (Normal Users)** | ❌ NO | Never | Not needed for platform operation |
| **IP Address (Threat Actors)** | ✅ YES | Upon abuse detection | Blocking, threat intelligence, law enforcement handover |
| **Message Content (Normal Users)** | ❌ NO | Never | Your conversations are yours |
| **Message Content (Abuse Reports)** | ✅ YES | When reported | Evidence preservation, investigation |
| **Email Address** | ⚠️ OPTIONAL | Account recovery only | You can use pseudonymous email services |
| **Payment Information** | ⚠️ LIMITED | During transactions | Processed by payment processor, not stored by us |
| **Device Fingerprint** | ❌ NO | Never | Unnecessary for platform function |
| **Browser Type/OS** | ⚠️ AGGREGATED | Anonymous analytics | Improving platform performance (no individual tracking) |
| **Usage Patterns** | ⚠️ AGGREGATED | Anonymous analytics | Understanding feature usage (no individual tracking) |

## 🛡️ WHAT WE LOG (Threat-Only Logging)

We maintain logs **only** for:

| Log Category | What's Recorded | Retention |
|--------------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Abuse Reports** | Reported user ID, evidence, timestamps | Until resolved + 30 days |
| **Blocked Threat Actors** | Discord ID/Username, threat score, blacklist status | Permanent (threat intelligence) |
| **Anomaly Detection** | Pattern data, not personal identifiers | 90 days |
| **Security Incidents** | Technical forensic data | As required by law |

**We do NOT log:**
- Normal user message history
- Normal user IP addresses  
- Normal user browsing behavior
- Normal user connections/timestamps

## 🔒 PAYMENT DATA

When you make payments on Open Tenebris:

| What We Do | What We Don't Do |
|------------|------------------|
| Process through encrypted payment gateway | Store credit card numbers |
| Retain transaction ID for support | Store CVV codes |
| Offer cryptocurrency options for privacy | Link payment to your platform activity |

**Cryptocurrency payments leave no trail to your identity.** This is by design.

## 👁️ THIRD PARTIES & DATA SHARING

| Recipient | What We Share | When |
|-----------|---------------|------|
| **Law Enforcement** | Verified threat actor data, abuse evidence | With legal process or emergency request |
| **HavenWatch Foundation** | Child exploitation reports | Immediately (via lawful reporting pipeline) |
| **Payment Processors** | Transaction data only | During payment processing |
| **Cloudflare** | Edge traffic data (anonymized) | Real-time (DDoS protection) |
| **Other Users** | Nothing unless you choose to share | N/A |

**We do NOT sell user data. We do NOT share data with advertisers. We do NOT have advertisers.**

## 🧠 YOUR RIGHTS

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Access your data** | Contact us with your user ID (no email needed) |
| **Delete your account** | Use the account deletion feature in settings |
| **Opt out of all non-essential collection** | You already have — there is no non-essential collection |

## 📅 DATA RETENTION SUMMARY

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Normal user activity | **Never logged** |
| Abuse evidence | Until resolved + 30 days |
| Threat intelligence (blacklisted IDs) | Permanent (public benefit) |
| Anomaly detection patterns | 90 days |
| Payment transaction IDs | 7 years (legal requirement) |

## 🚀 WHY THIS POLICY EXISTS

We built Open Tenebris because major platforms:

- Log every message you've ever sent
- Track every IP address you've ever used
- Sell your data to advertisers
- Keep your data forever, even after account deletion
- Can't tell the difference between a normal user and a threat actor

**We can.**

Our logging stops when normal behavior is detected. It only begins when something threatens the community.

## ⚖️ LEGAL BASIS (GDPR/CCPA Compliance)

| Processing Activity | Legal Basis |
|---------------------|-------------|
| Abuse detection & blocking | Legitimate interest (community safety) |
| Threat intelligence database | Public interest (security research) |
| Payment processing | Contract fulfillment |
| Anonymous analytics | Legitimate interest (platform improvement) |
| Law enforcement handover | Legal obligation |

**We do not rely on "consent" for data collection because we don't collect normal user data in the first place.**
